Entrepreneurial practice

Faiez Ghorbel

Date
Jul 2, 2022 9:00 AM
Event
DES-RU
Location
Amphi A9 - Faculty of science - University of Sfax
A workshop will be organized by the Data Engineering and Semantics research unit at the Faculty of Sciences of Sfax. The training covers the foundations of entrepreneurial practice. As a multidisciplinary phenomenon, business models play an important role in the creation of entrepreneurial value. This training highlights the main practices that can facilitate the creation of an entrepreneurial project.
Faiez Ghorbel
Researcher in entrepreneurship

co-founder of the Djagora foundation in charge of entrepreneurial development and strategic development facilitator (level 2).
